The Call of the Stone Heart

From the depths of the oldest world, covered in moss and roots, Nedrianna Mirra rises - the Goddess of the Earth.
Her gaze is as deep as rocks, and her voice is like the roar of ancient caves. She preserves memories of the birth of mountains and the sprouting of the first leaf. Her strength is stability, power and endurance.
Nedrianna Mirra tests not the strength of her hands, but the depth of understanding of what gives life to everything.
To receive a share of her power, complete the task she has prepared. The correct answer will allow you to take the crystal of earthly wisdom and move further - towards a new element.
