The Secret of Per Krechet

Dear friend!
Welcome to the Magical World of Ellaria!
Ellaria is our fairyland, powered by the energy of kind hearts from your real world. We cannot exist without each other.
But the balance is broken... The Dark Force has awakened and threatens to erase Ellaria from the face of the dimension. All hope is only in you...

The wise men of our world suddenly disappeared and the fire of the Book of Life went out.
The Feather of Krechet is an artifact created by the mighty bird-dragon Krechet, a being who once united light and darkness. It was with his Feather that the wise men of our world wrote protective spells in the Magic Book of Life - a source of wisdom, harmony and strength.
Noticing the threat, they managed to disperse the power of the Feather between the four Guardians - the Goddesses of Earth, Water, Wind and Fire.
Only the one who passes their tests will be able to collect parts of the power, restore the Feather and stop the darkness.

The evil Ruin has long dreamed that one day he would be able to take over our world and commit atrocities in your world.
Hoping to be able to take over the Feather of Krechet, he kidnapped our sages.
But he did not know that the power of the universe follows the balance and when threatened with destruction, the Feather loses its magical power.
Now the Feather of Krechet is waiting for the return of its magical properties and reunion with the sages to create the most powerful spell against Ruin.

To prevent Ruin from seizing power in the Universe, you can help us.
He is playing with everyone from our world, but he cannot see you. Your world is closed to him for now.
Only you can return the power of Per Krechet and save both of our worlds!
We will send you through a time-space tunnel, through which you will reach the Goddesses Guardians of the four elements: Nedrianna Mirra (Goddess of Earth), Vodelia Silva (Goddess of Water), Palanna Ardent (Goddess of Fire) and Vitrelia Bollei (Goddess of Wind).
You will need to take turns passing the tests of the Goddesses for observation, intelligence and intelligence.
Legend has it that only the worthy will be able to restore the power of Per Krechet.
We are sure that it is you!
